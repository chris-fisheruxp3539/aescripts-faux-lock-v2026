## Overview

Aescripts Faux Lock is a workflow-oriented plugin for Adobe After Effects that makes it easy to safeguard chosen layers during editing. It is built for motion design work where timelines can get crowded, precision matters, and one stray change can disrupt a carefully assembled composition.

With a quick way to temporarily protect layers, the plugin helps keep projects orderly and editing sessions focused. It fits well when you are moving between animation stages, refining smaller details, or working in scenes where certain elements need to stay fixed while the rest of the comp evolves.

## Usage

1. Open your After Effects project.
2. Choose the layers you want to keep temporarily protected.
3. Apply Faux Lock to prevent those layers from being edited while you work on the rest of the composition.
4. Turn off the protection when you are ready to edit those layers again.

Typical workflow:
- Protect background or completed elements
- Adjust foreground animation without affecting locked layers
- Re-enable editing when revisions call for changes
